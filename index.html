I am a research scholar in the Computer Science Group at Chennai Mathematical Institute (CMI), working with Professor Samir Datta.

I am broadly interested in theoretical aspects of computer science. Currently, the focus of my research has been on dynamic graph problems (graph isomorphism, group isomorphism, graph embedding etc.) from the persepcetive descriptive complexity. I am also interested in algebraic techniques for algorithm, exact exponential algorithms, and algorithmic group theory.

Before this, I did my masters in Computer Science at CMI and B.Tech. in Computer Engineering at AMU.

On the job market for postdoctoral positions starting January 2026.
