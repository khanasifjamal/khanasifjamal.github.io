I am a research scholar in the Computer Science Group at Chennai Mathematical Institute (CMI), working with Professor Samir Datta. I am broadly interested in theoretical aspects of computer science.

Currently, focus of my research has been in the areas of dynamic descriptive complexity, dynamic graph algorithms, exact exponential algorithms and algorithmic group theory.
